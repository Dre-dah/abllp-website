Alex Mbaya

Alex Mbaya is a Partner at the firm. Alex graduated in 2019 from Kenyatta University School of Law where he attained second-class honors, upper division. He undertook the Advocates Training Programme at the Kenya School of Law from 2020 to 2021. He was admitted to the bar in 2022 as an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya. Alex is a member of the Law Society of Kenya. Alex attended KCA University in 2020 where he completed the Certified Secretarial course administered by KASNEB. Alex is a Company Secretary and a member of the Institute of Certified Secretaries of Kenya (ICSK).

Alex has extensive experience in the areas of dispute resolution and real estate and finance, providing clients with effective and practical solutions to a wide range of legal challenges in these areas.

Alex also has expertise in corporate and commercial law and is also particularly skilled in advising clients on company formation and incorporation, corporate governance, and company secretarial work. His background as a company secretary has given him a deep understanding of the legal and regulatory requirements that apply to businesses in Kenya and he is well-equipped to help clients navigate the complex landscape of corporate and commercial law.

In the area of intellectual property, Alex has extensive experience advising clients on all aspects of intellectual property law, including trademark registration, patent application and prosecution, and copyright infringement. He is well-equipped to help clients protect and enforce their intellectual property rights in Kenya and to provide guidance on the most effective strategies for managing and monetizing these assets.

Boniface Mawira

Boniface Mawira is a Partner at the firm. Boniface graduated in 2019 from Kenyatta University School of Law where he attained second-class honors, upper division. Boniface undertook the Advocates Training Programme at the Kenya School of Law from 2020 to 2021. He was admitted to the bar in 2022 as an Advocate of the High Court of Kenya. Boniface is a member of the Law Society of Kenya.

Boniface brings a wealth of experience and knowledge to the firm. He is highly regarded for his ability to provide practical and effective solutions to complex legal problems and for his commitment to protecting our clients' interests.

In the area of dispute resolution, Boniface is highly regarded for his ability to advise and represent clients in sensitive and/or high-profile matters before Kenyan courts and alternative dispute resolution mechanisms, including arbitral tribunals and mediation. He is known for his strong negotiation skills and his ability to provide practical and effective solutions to complex legal problems.

In the area of real estate and finance, Boniface has extensive experience advising clients on a wide range of legal and regulatory issues, including property transactions, property development, banking and finance, and landlord and tenant disputes. He is well-versed in the legal and regulatory requirements that apply to real estate transactions in Kenya and has a deep understanding of the complex issues that arise in these areas.
