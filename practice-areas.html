Real Estate and Finance

At A&B Advocates LLP, our commitment to excellence extends to our Real Estate and Finance practice area. We have earned a distinguished reputation as a premier provider of legal services in this field within the region.

Our Real Estate and Finance practice encompasses both advisory and transactional services, catering to a diverse clientele, including esteemed banks and financial institutions, forward-thinking property developers, discerning landlords and tenants, government bodies, and private individuals.

Our seasoned legal experts bring profound knowledge and experience to every facet of real estate transactions, ranging from acquisitions and sales to leasing, financing, development, and zoning. Our strength lies in delivering practical and innovative solutions to intricate real estate and finance matters, ensuring a seamless journey from due diligence to closing.

Moreover, our attorneys boast a rich history of successful engagements in advisory and transactional work, navigating complex negotiations and disputes with precision. We pride ourselves on crafting tailored approaches that align with our clients' unique requirements, fostering enduring client relationships built on trust and respect.

Dispute Resolution

A&B Advocates LLP is widely recognized for its prowess in Dispute Resolution. Our practice is distinguished by its ability to offer expert guidance and representation in sensitive and high-profile cases, encompassing Kenyan courts and alternative dispute resolution avenues, such as arbitral tribunals and mediation.

Our Dispute Resolution team boasts extensive experience in navigating clients through a broad spectrum of disputes, including those of a commercial, contractual, construction, employment, intellectual property, and real estate nature. We excel in delivering practical and effective solutions to intricate legal challenges, marked by efficiency and effectiveness.

Our legal professionals are accomplished negotiators and litigators, boasting a remarkable track record of securing favorable outcomes for our clients. We are committed to safeguarding our clients' interests and collaborate closely with them to devise customized strategies tailored to each unique case.

Corporate, Commercial, and Intellectual Property

Our firm offers comprehensive legal support to corporations and individuals engaged in corporate and commercial activities. We possess substantial experience in advising clients across a spectrum of corporate and commercial matters, earning a reputation as trusted legal advisors.

The Corporate, Commercial, and Intellectual Property practice area provides a full suite of services, including company formation and incorporation, corporate governance, joint ventures, financing, and intellectual property protection and enforcement.

Our legal team demonstrates a profound understanding of the intricate legal and regulatory landscape that governs businesses in Kenya. We are adept at guiding clients through the complexities of corporate and commercial law, ensuring legal compliance and fostering successful business ventures.

Furthermore, our esteemed company secretarial team delivers meticulous services, encompassing the preparation and filing of annual returns, meticulous maintenance of statutory registers, and the preparation of precise minutes and resolutions. Their unwavering commitment to accuracy and timeliness is renowned among our clients.
